WHAT IS POP & LOCK TECH?

Pop: A sharp muscular contraction at the precise beat onset — scored by how closely your joint-velocity spike aligns to the beat marker (within ±80ms = on-beat).

Lock: A deliberate freeze/hold at the beat resolution — scored by joint-velocity falling below threshold within ±120ms of target beat.

Remix: Your recorded session is replayed against a 100% template ghost showing what perfect beat-sync execution of the same routine would look like. The delta is your improvement target.

WeCoin integration: Beat sync % contributes a multiplier to your daily PR mineability. 100% beat sync = maximum coin reward per PR.
